# Anomalous Restarts

### Introduction 
Even though we point at our computers and glorify them as a symbol of our advancement amd human development; Computers are in reality dumb. 

The key takeaway here that we need to understand is that computers are incredibly powerful and fast at performing "given" tasks. They don't think on their own neither our computers perform tasks on their own. We tell them to do something, we guide them from A to B; the computer finds the fastest route between those two points.

We, as humans are yet to create a machine that is sentient and has consciousness of it's own. It's a story and a topic for another day.
